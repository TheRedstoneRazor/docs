title: "Mobs"
---

Many mobs have been tweaked, primarily with the goal of providing more challenge in the lategame.

As well as reworked existing mobs, new mobs have been introduced for unique challenges, especially when exploring the deepest parts of the world and the End.

<Info>
  Mob stats assume the **Hard** difficulty.
</Info>

# Changed Mobs

- **Cave Spider:** Now rarely spawns below Y0.
- **Blaze:** Now has 30 HP.
- **Wither Skeleton:** Now has 40 HP.
- **Evoker:** Now **use** their totems during raids. To get one for yourself, you'll need to invade a Woodland Mansion or Mage Tower.
- **Drowned:** Can no longer drop tridents. Elder Guardians are the new source for these.
- **Phantom:** No longer spawns in the Overworld, instead encountered in Toxic Heaps and Shadowlands. Phantoms encountered in the End have 40 HP and deal 5 hearts of damage.

# New Mobs

### Cave Zombie

**Location:** Below Y0\
**Health:** 26\
**Armour:** 4\
**Attack:** 6

### Cave Skeleton

**Location:** Below Y0\
**Health:** 26\
**Health:** 4-8 (Bow), inflicts Weakness

Zombies and skeletons encountered in the deepest parts of the world are tougher than those on the surface.

### Shade

**Location:** Deep Dark\
**Health:** 40\
**Attack:** 9

Shades are lost souls left to wander eternally in the darkness. These creatures are silent and completely invisible unless brought near a light source.

Shades ensure the Deep Dark does not become a safe haven once sculk shriekers are removed. They despawn when the Warden is summoned.

### Ender Stalker

**Location:** The End\
**Health:** 40\
**Attack:** 8-16 (Bow)\
**Drops:** Glowstone Dust, Ender Pearl

Ender Stalkers pose a ranged threat in the End, firing burning arrows from a long distance that inflict blindness.

Ender Stalkers can be encountered anywhere in the dimension except for the central island, but are most commonly found guarding end cities.

They have projectile protection, taking reduced damage from arrows themselves and will teleport away if a player gets too close.

# Bosses

Minecraft's bosses have been buffed to make them far more formidable opponents. Prepare well for these battles, as they will test you to the limit.

## Undead Warrior

**Health:** 50\
**Armour:** 16\
**Attack:** 10

A fallen champion of battle. Now reanimated, its muscle memory reawakens and it fights tirelessly. These are rarely found in place of a normal zombie throughout the world.

Drops an enchanted book on defeat.

**Block:** The Undead Warrior raises its shield, protecting it from damage.\
**Dash:** The Undead Warrior rapidly dashes towards its target.

## Nethersent

**Health:** 100\
**Attack:** 10

An ancient construct tasked with guarding the seal between realms. Upon attempting to open a portal for the first time, the Nethersent will be summoned.

**Earthquake:** Delivers a massive shockwave to the ground, damaging any nearby creatures.\
**Fireball:** Hurls a fireball at its target, creating a small explosion and lighting the ground on fire.

Once below half health, it leaves a trail of fire wherever it walks and uses its fireball attack three times in quick succession.

## Elder Guardian

**Health:** 80\
**Attack:** 12

Elder Guardians now drop a shard of Elder Prismarine on death. Three of these shards can be combined to create a trident.

## The Wither

**Health:** 600\
**Attack:** Varies\
\
The Wither now roughly matches its Bedrock Edition counterpart, porting all moves over from that version.

If trapped in bedrock or another block it cannot break, the Wither will teleport to escape.

The Wither drops an enchanted Soulbound book alongside the Nether Star, allowing you to make an item stay in your inventory on death.

## The Ender Dragon

**Health:** 500\
**Attack:** Varies

<Danger>
  The Ender Dragon is intended to be tackled as a multiplayer challenge. Attempting to defeat it on your own is ill-advised.
</Danger>

The Ender Dragon battle now features an enhanced arsenal of abilities to test you and your allies.

The dragon is now **immune** to explosions, rendering bed and respawn anchor strategies unviable.

The dragon can spawn mobs and aggro Endermen towards players. Crystals will also slowly respawn over the course of the battle.
