Some recipes and outputs have been changed for balance or where we believe a vanilla recipe can be improved.

A few entirely new items and recipes have been added to spice up survival and keep things fresh, or fill in gaps in vanilla Minecraft's feature set.

# Changed Recipes

- Chain: Now outputs 3
- Trapdoors: Now outputs 6
- Bucket: Now crafted using copper
- Polished Deepslate: Now crafted using regular deepslate (instead of cobbled)
- Button/Pressure Plate/Lever: Now require 1 redstone

# New Recipes

- Mangrove Roots: Can be crafted into 4 sticks
- Mud: Can be smelted to produce Dirt
- Raw Ore Blocks: Can be blasted to directly produce Metal Blocks (Copper, Gold, Zinc)

## Stone Consistency

These changes have been made pursuit of consistency among the crafting stones so that stone, deepslate and blackstone follow the same rules in regards to smelting and crafting.

### Cobbled Blackstone

Blackstone now has a cobbled variant dropped upon being mined. Cobbled blackstone is used for making tools, while regular blackstone can be obtained via silk touch or smelting in a furnace.

Regular blackstone, along with regular deepslate are required to craft their respective polished and brick variants for consistency with crafting using regular stone.

### Stone Substitution

Most recipes that previously demanded cobblestone will now accept cobbled deepslate and cobbled blackstone. Regular stone can now also be substituted with deepslate or blackstone.
