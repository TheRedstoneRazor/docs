Changes have been made to various existing structures to give them new purpose, giving players more reasons to challenge these locations.

# Overworld

## Dungeon

Dungeons are now much larger and more varied in design and layout.

Chests in dungeons can now contain some specific potions, allowing a way to get these items as valuable consumables before brewing becomes available.

## Trial Chambers

Trial Chambers are no longer made out of copper blocks, so they cannot be harvested for near infinite copper.

Changes to Trial Chambers from 1.21.2 have also been backported.

## Ocean Monument

Monuments have been re-envisioned as the new source for the trident.

Drowneds can no longer drop tridents. Instead, each Elder Guardian drops 1 elder prismarine, which is used in a new crafting recipe:

## Woodland Mansion

The loot available in mansions has been greatly buffed to reflect the difficulty of these structures. Forge rooms, dining halls, libraries and storerooms have all been given new custom loot to uncover.

Evokers have wisened up a little and will now **use** their totems during raids. If you want to get one, you'll need to invade their home turf. Fight an Evoker in a Mansion or Mage Tower and you can claim a totem for yourself.

### Ancient Explorer Maps

Unlike other maps, Ancient Explorer Maps can only be found stowed away in the map rooms within woodland mansions, pointing towards the long lost Ancient City.

## Ancient City

Ancient City chests have an increased chance of carrying Mending and Soulbound books.

### Shades

The Deep Dark is now inhabited by lost souls eternally wandering in the darkness. Shades will automatically despawn if the Warden is summoned.

These new mobs ensure the Deep Dark does not become a safe haven once the sculk shriekers are removed.

## Stronghold

Strongholds are now much larger and have more varied loot.

The Eye of Ender recipe has been changed to require a beacon. For more information see **\<WIP\>**

# The Nether

## Nether Fortress

Fortresses have been changed to reduce the grindy nature of obtaining Wither Skeleton Skulls.

Wither Skeletons now have 40 HP and only drop skulls when killed with a trident. The drop rate has been increased to 12.5%.

Blazes now have 30 HP.
