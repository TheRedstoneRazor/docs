These rules apply across our network and exist to keep Trixtail Tavern safe, fair and enjoyable for everyone.

If you've been punished in any of our communities and would like to appeal, [please click here](https://forms.gle/fcyhEkApngp4UkZc7).

## Rules Handbook

This handbook is intended as a quick rules reference and is not a replacement for our full rule list.

- Be nice, be considerate, and use common sense
- Don't spam or otherwise abuse public chat
- Swearing is allowed, but don't get excessive
- Avoid discussion of potentially controversial or insensitive topics
- Don't advertise without prior staff approval
- Don't post or create inappropriate or explicit content
- Don't hack, exploit or abuse mechanics on our game servers

## Class I Violations

_Typical Punishment: Warning/Temporary Mute_

<Info>
  For some class I violations you may be first issued an informal warning which is not logged. Abuse of this system will likely prevent you from receiving these in the future. Informal warnings will not be issued if you have any previous logged punishments.
</Info>

**Misconduct:** Presenting yourself in a way that is discomforting for other users. This includes:

- Toxic behaviour
- Excessive swearing
- Excessive negativity
- Rudeness/disrespect
- Flirting or e-dating
- Hostility towards other users

Trixtail is a communal space and we want to maintain a positive atmosphere where everyone can get along.

**Misusing chat:** Using public chat in any way that is disruptive for other users. This includes:

- Spamming (nonsensical or rapid-fire messages)
- Zalgo (irregular Unicode characters / 𝓯𝓪𝓷𝓬𝔂 𝓽𝓮𝔁𝓽)
- Pinging users unnecessarily and attempting to use @everyone and @here
- Discussion of potentially controversial or insensitive topics (including but not limited to politics, religion, criminal activity)
- Bypassing the language filter
- Speaking in a language other than English (Our community and moderation staff are predominantly English and it is difficult to moderate chat in other languages)

**Misusing voice chat:** Using voice chat in any disruptive way not covered by the prior rules. This includes:

- Excessive background noise (If this is unavoidable, please enable push to talk)
- Interrupting other users
- Excessively loud volume
- Broadcasting explicit or inappropriate audio

## Class II Violations

_Typical Punishment: Temporary Mute/Ban_

**Harassment/Bullying:** Intentionally trying to make others' experience on Trixtail Tavern less enjoyable. This includes:

- Discrimination
- Trolling
- Griefing
- Threats
- Impersonation

In severe cases, this rule may be elevated to Class III.

**Advertising:** Posting links or search terms to things not related to the server for personal or commercial gain.

Sharing a song or video you found does not violate this rule. This rule covers actions such as asking people to follow you on social media, buy a product or join another server.

**Incitement:** Encouraging other users to violate rules.

## Class III Violations

_Typical Punishment: Permanent Ban_

**Hacking/Exploiting:** Abusing mods, clients or unintended bugs on our game servers to gain an unfair advantage.

<Info>
  If you plan on playing on our game servers using either your own modded client or a modified version of the server modpack, we **strongly advise** that you consult a staff member to verify that your modifications are permissible.
</Info>

**Inappropriate Content:** Creating or posting digital content of an offensive, gory, sexual or otherwise inappropriate nature. This includes avatars, usernames, media and digital creations.

(In the case of an avatar or username, you do not have to wait to appeal as long as you have changed it.)

**Contempt:** Showing obvious disregard for community rules or property. This includes committing multiple class I-II violations within minutes of initially joining.

## Serious Violations

<Danger>
  The following will always result in a **permanent, non-appealable IP-Ban** from all services associated with Trixtail Tavern.
</Danger>

**Chargeback:** Opening a chargeback on any purchase made on our online store. Ingame purchases made on Trixtail Tavern are strictly non-refundable. If you encounter any issues making an online purchase, please contact an administrator.

**Breaking the Law:** Using our services to facilitate any form of criminal activity. Your IP address and any other details on record may be shared with local law enforcement.

**Evasion:** Using alternate accounts or any other means to evade punishments.

## Additional Information

At any class (excluding Serious Violations) staff may choose to issue you with a formal Warning instead of the punishment typical of that violation at their discretion. Such instances are decided on a case by case basis and are typically only granted on the merit of prior exemplary behaviour or an violation being considered borderline to having been committed.

Lack of knowledge of the rules is not considered a mitigating circumstance.

### Appeals

Any mute or non-IP ban can be [appealed here](https://forms.gle/fcyhEkApngp4UkZc7). The amount of time that has elapsed since the rule violation took place will be strongly considered when determining the outcome of your appeal.

Please note that IP bans **cannot** be appealed.

#### Outcomes

Following an appeal submission, the following outcomes are possible:

- **No change.** The information provided in the appeal was insufficient or invalid.
- **A reduction in the punishment duration.** The information provided demonstrates that the individual has appropriately reflected on their actions and/or mitigating circumstances were involved or the punishment was excessive for the nature of the rule violation.
- **The punishment being altered to a formal warning.** The information provided demonstrates that the individual has thoroughly reflected on their actions and/or significant mitigating circumstances were involved.
- **The punishment being revoked.** This outcome can only happen if, based on information provided, there is insufficient evidence that a rule violation took place. In this case, the punishment will be removed from your record.

If you are unhappy with the outcome of your appeal, you may submit another appeal one month after your previous submission. When submitting subsequent appeals, you should consider why your previous appeal was denied.

If a ban is successfully appealed and you are banned again, any remaining time on your original ban will be added to your ban period. This does not apply if the original ban was revoked.

### Conduct Agreement

By joining Trixtail Tavern, you agree that you will follow all of these rules at all times. Failure to comply may lead to your account being restricted from accessing our services.

You are responsible for all activity on your account. As such, having a friend, family member or anyone else break a rule on your account will still lead to that account being punished.

You are expected to follow the ‘spirit’ of these rules as opposed to the exact wording. As such, there are no ‘loopholes’. Staff members have the final verdict and you are expected to abide by their requests to the same degree as you would to this document.

## General Disclaimer

This document is not binding and staff may, under rare circumstances, restrict access on any or all platforms for reasons unstated within this document. This is usually only done in the interest of community safety and whether we deem that you may not be a good fit for our community.

By interacting on Trixtail Tavern or otherwise accessing our services, you consent to being monitored at any point by server staff with or without your knowledge for the purposes of safeguarding, moderation and staff training.
